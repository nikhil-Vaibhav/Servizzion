
SERVIZZION

It is a mobile application where business can look for freelance services and sellers can sell their services in a very easy-to-use and simple manner. The application has a very clean UI. A new user can also open an account and start selling.
Another advantage is that the commission is very less as compared to other websites. You get to keep 95% of your earnings with you.
